## More information about matrices

#### Order of matrices
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3      \\
2 & n.. & n..  \\
3 & n.. & n..
\end{bmatrix}
$$
$$
3 \times 3=9 \text{ order matrix}
$$

$$\Large m\times n$$


> where $$m$$ is number of **rows** and $$n$$ is number of **columns**

> Two matrices are considered equal if both have same order and there components are same

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4
\end{bmatrix}
$$
